Dungeon Sweep

Clear the dungeon one room at a time. Safe rooms show nearby trap counts, treasure rooms award coins, and scouting lets you check danger before you commit.

How To Play

  1. Click a room to reveal it. Numbers show how many traps touch that room.
  2. Empty safe rooms expand automatically, just like classic Minesweeper.
  3. Trap rooms cost 1 HP instead of ending the run immediately.
  4. Treasure rooms reward coins when you reveal them.
  5. After your first move, Scout can inspect one hidden room for 2 coins.
  6. Reveal every safe room before your HP reaches zero to win the run.

Controls

  • Click hidden rooms to reveal them.
  • Turn on Flag Mode to place or remove flags with a click.
  • On desktop, right-click also toggles a flag.
  • Scout inspects one hidden room, then switches itself off.
